What is Somnia?

Somnia is a sleep app developed by Raajvir Vijay.

Click start and follow the instructions. The app will project a circle on the ceiling that slowly expands, and you should synchronize your breathing with it. Once the timer stops, the app will automatically close and go to sleep.

Visit somnia.app for more information.
